About Me

The Pen Behind
The Poetry

I am a passionate writer who believes that words have the power to heal, inspire, and transform. From the tender verses of poetry to the vivid worlds of short stories and novels, my writing captures the raw beauty of human emotions. Every shayari I pen carries a piece of my soul, every story a fragment of my imagination.

My journey with words began as a child, scribbling thoughts in worn-out notebooks. Today, those scribbles have blossomed into a garden of literary expressions — from heartfelt poetry that speaks of love and longing, to captivating stories that transport readers to another world, and profound shayaris that echo the depth of the heart.

Through this space, I invite you to dive into my world of words. Read, feel, reflect — and if my words touch your soul, let me know. Every comment, every like, is a conversation between two kindred spirits.
